The reasons to save water
The main reason to save water is that only 1 percent of water in earth is drinkable, and another 99% is sea water. If we lack of water, it means that there is a possibility that the humanity would end. In addition, we won't have enough foods or crops to eat as well.
African boy, who is washing his face with water
Saving water - means saving money as well. It takes a lot of time to make the water into a clean water that is drinkable, such as desallianation from sea water, and we must try to save water as much as possible.
